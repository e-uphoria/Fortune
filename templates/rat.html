  • Securing Finances: The energy of the Snake will be for financial security; thus, this is a good year for consolidating finances. Now is the time to be resourceful in handling your finances- manage money wisely, save strategically, and make selected investments that will pay dividends in the long run.

    • Challenges for Rats in 2025

    • Competition: The Year of the Snake may bring heightened competition, which can be motivating and stressful. Collaboration and teamwork will yield better results than confrontation.
